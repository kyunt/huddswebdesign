What Happens Once You Order?

  1. Initial Contract

    We send over an initial contract for you to sign. Once this is done, we bill you the first invoice (50%).

  2. Business Information

    Once the first invoice is paid, we will ask you for information about your business. We'll need any logos and pictures you want to be on the website.

  3. Design

    We'll send over some websites that we think will be a fit. Once you have a look at those and tell us what you like and don't like, we'll start to work on your website design.

  4. Copywriting

    We will loop in our copywriter to research keywords for your website and write the content.

  5. Development

    When you're happy with our design and we've made any changes you have requested, we'll get to work coding your website, ensuring it fits on every screen size and scores a high Google Page Speed score.

  6. Delivery

    When everything is finished, we put it all together. We will send you a link to the demo and make sure you approve of it. At this point, we invoice you the remaining amount, and the subsequent monthly fees for hosting and maintenance.

This whole process should take less than a week, provided we receive everything we ask from you in a timely manner.
